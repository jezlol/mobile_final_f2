This directory contains screenshots of the application.

Please replace the placeholder files with actual screenshots of your application:

1. home_screen.png - Screenshot of the main screen of the application
2. add_sale.png - Screenshot of the screen for adding a new sale
3. sales_details.png - Screenshot of the screen showing detailed information about a sale

To take screenshots on your device:
- Android: Press and hold the Power and Volume Down buttons simultaneously
- iOS: Press the Side button and Volume Up button simultaneously
- Emulator/Simulator: Use the screenshot tool in your development environment

After taking screenshots, place them in this directory with the appropriate names.
